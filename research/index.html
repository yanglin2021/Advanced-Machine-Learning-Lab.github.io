研究方向

Learning Theory

  • Online Learning: Experts, Bandits, Online Convex Optimization, Multi-scale Online Learing，Online Learning with Attacks/Corruptions
  • Distributed Learning/Estimation

Online Optimization

  • Online Knapsack Problems: Online Multidimensional Knapsack, Online Multiple Knapsack, Online Knapsack with Departure etc.
  • Online Linear Programming, Online Buffer Management

Networking

  • Software Defined Networking, Cloud Computing, Data Centers, etc.
  • Performance Analysis, Modeling, and Online Resource Optimization for Networked Computing Systems
